### Table 1: Molecular Subtypes Summary

| Subtype | Biomarkers | Characteristics | Preferred Therapy | Prognosis |
| ------- | ---------- | --------------- | ----------------- | --------- |
| **Luminal A** | ER+, PR+, HER2-, low Ki-67 | Slow-growing, highly hormone sensitive | Endocrine therapy | Favorable |
| **Luminal B** | ER+, PR+/-, HER2+/-, high Ki-67 | Faster growing, intermediate hormone sensitivity | Endocrine therapy ± Targeted/Chemotherapy | Intermediate |
| **HER2-Enriched** | ER-, PR-, HER2+ | Aggressive growth, high expression of HER2 pathway | Anti-HER2 targeted therapy + Chemotherapy | Improved survival (due to targeted therapies) |
| **TNBC** | ER-, PR-, HER2- | Highly aggressive, lacks standard receptors, high mutational burden | Chemotherapy + Immunotherapy (e.g., Pembrolizumab) | Aggressive disease, highest risk of early relapse |
| **HER2-Low** | IHC 1+ or 2+/ISH- | Clinically diverse, previously classified as HER2-negative | Antibody-Drug Conjugates (e.g., T-DXd) | Emerging category, varies by HR status |
