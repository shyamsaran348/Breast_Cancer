# Handover Context: Contemporary Breast Cancer Management Review

## 1. Project Goal
This repository contains a draft narrative review article targeted for submission to **Cancer Treatment Reviews (CTRC)** (or a similar high-impact journal). 
**Working Title:** *Contemporary Breast Cancer Management in the Era of Precision Oncology: Therapeutic Innovations, Barriers to Care, and Future Directions*.

## 2. Current State of the Manuscript
The manuscript has been developed through several iterative phases of strict scientific auditing and expansion. 
- **Word Count:** Currently sitting at ~4,200 dense, highly concentrated words (excluding tables and references).
- **Content Structure:**
  - `Manuscript_Draft_Part1.md`: Covers Introduction, Molecular Landscape, and Diagnostics (AI, Radiomics, Risk-Stratified Screening).
  - `Manuscript_Draft_Part2.md`: Covers Treatment Strategies (Surgical De-escalation, Radiation Advances) and Emerging Innovations (ADCs, PARP inhibitors, CAR-T, Bispecifics).
  - `Manuscript_Draft_Part3.md`: Covers Barriers to Care (Financial Toxicity, LMIC disparities) and Future Directions (Digital Twins, Adaptive Trials).
  - `Manuscript_Tables.md`: Contains 5 structured, journal-ready tables.
  - `References.md`: The finalized, sequentially numbered bibliography in Vancouver format.

## 3. Figures & Assets
We moved away from AI-generated (DALL-E) images and generated 5 publication-ready **Matplotlib** figures (`Figure1_Timeline.png` to `Figure5_Roadmap.png`). These are clean, reproducible, and reviewer-friendly.
